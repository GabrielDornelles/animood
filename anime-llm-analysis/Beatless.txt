In a near-future society where human-like robots, known as hIEs, blur the lines between companionship and servitude, *Beatless* explores profound themes of identity, autonomy, and the essence of humanity. The narrative centers around Arato Endou, a kind-hearted high school student who, despite his financial struggles, treats these artificial beings as equals. His life takes a dramatic turn when a routine errand becomes a life-and-death struggle, leading him to a fateful encounter with Lacia, a weaponized hIE that challenges his perceptions of existence and emotion.

The emotional landscape of *Beatless* is rich with tension and philosophical inquiry. Arato's relationship with Lacia serves as a poignant exploration of trust and responsibility, inviting viewers to ponder the implications of technology on human connections.
