This season deepens the exploration of their friendship, revealing the weight of responsibility and the bonds forged in adversity, echoing themes found in series like "Mob Psycho 100" and "Bleach."

Fans of character-driven narratives will appreciate the evolving dynamics between the protagonists and their adversaries, as well as the rich world-building that paints a vivid picture of the supernatural landscape.

This anime resonates with those seeking a blend of adventure, humor, and emotional depth, making it ideal for viewers in a curious or contemplative mood.
