The pacing balances moments of introspection with the frenetic energy of school life, creating an atmosphere rife with both tension and comedy. As Hyung Suk navigates the pitfalls of popularity, he learns that external validation is fraught with its own pitfalls, echoing the philosophical inquiries of self-acceptance and the quest for genuine relationships.
