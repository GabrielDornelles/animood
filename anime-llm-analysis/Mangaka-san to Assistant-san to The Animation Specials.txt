This collection of shorts encapsulates the comedic absurdities and awkward dynamics inherent in the creative process, particularly through the lens of Aito Yuuki, a mangaka struggling to grasp the emotional depth of his characters. The humor often veers into the absurd, showcasing the lengths to which Ashisu Sahoto, his assistant, will go to help him connect with his work, including hilariously inappropriate scenarios that challenge boundaries and provoke laughter.
