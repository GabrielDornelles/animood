The atmosphere is electric, filled with frenetic energy that mirrors the chaotic lives of the characters, particularly the protagonist, Negishi, whose struggle between passion for music and personal identity resonates deeply.

This anime is perfect for those seeking a wild ride that challenges conventional norms, all while delivering a catchy soundtrack that encapsulates the spirit of metal music.
