The narrative centers on Kouta, a shy country boy whose life spirals into chaos when he becomes the object of affection for various animal spirits, particularly Chizuru, a captivating fox spirit, and Nozomu, a fierce wolf spirit. This unusual love triangle infuses the story with a playful tension, merging lighthearted humor with moments of genuine emotional depth.

The atmosphere is infused with a blend of innocence and mischief, as Kouta navigates the complexities of high school life while grappling with his unexpected supernatural admirers. The pacing oscillates between comedic escapades and tender interactions, creating a rhythm that resonates with viewers seeking both laughter and heartfelt connection.
