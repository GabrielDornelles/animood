The return of familiar faces, including Heiji Hattori and Kaitou Kid, adds layers of camaraderie and rivalry, inviting fans to reflect on the bonds formed through shared struggles. The film's emotional tone oscillates between moments of lightheartedness and the gravity of impending danger, resonating with viewers who seek a blend of thrill and emotional depth.
