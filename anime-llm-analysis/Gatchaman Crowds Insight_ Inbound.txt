The pacing oscillates between frenetic action and contemplative moments, creating a rhythm that mirrors the viewer's own emotional state. This anime resonates particularly well with those feeling overwhelmed by societal pressures, or anyone seeking a thoughtful exploration of modern life’s complexities.
