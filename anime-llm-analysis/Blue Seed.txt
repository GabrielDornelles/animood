The story revolves around Momiji Fujimiya, an unassuming middle school girl who unexpectedly discovers her vital role as a descendant of the Kushinada clan. Her unique lineage positions her as humanity's last hope against the terrifying Aragami, monstrous entities rooted in Japanese folklore that threaten to engulf Japan in chaos. This fusion of action and myth creates an atmosphere steeped in tension, as Momiji grapples with her newfound identity while navigating the complexities of adolescence.

The narrative is punctuated by emotional highs and lows, capturing the struggle between duty and personal desire.
