The film's narrative follows Ruka, a girl escaping her turbulent home life through handball, only to find solace in an enchanting underwater world. Here, she meets Umi, a boy whose mystical connection to the ocean invites audiences to ponder the boundaries of humanity and nature. This dynamic trio, including Umi's brother Sora, navigates a vibrant yet haunting aquatic realm, where the sea itself emerges as a vital character, embodying both beauty and fragility.

The atmosphere is a kaleidoscope of vivid visuals and ethereal soundscapes, evoking feelings of wonder and introspection. The pacing oscillates between serene moments of discovery and intense emotional crescendos, creating a compelling juxtaposition that resonates with viewers seeking depth in their cinematic experiences.
