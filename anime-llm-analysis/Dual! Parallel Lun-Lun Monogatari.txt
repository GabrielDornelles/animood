This anime deftly navigates themes of escapism and self-discovery, appealing to those who resonate with the idea of finding purpose amidst confusion, reminiscent of titles like "Re:Creators" or "Digimon Tamers."

The pacing oscillates between lighthearted comedic moments and intense confrontations, evoking a sense of urgency and adventure that keeps viewers engaged.
