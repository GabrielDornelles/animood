This anime beautifully captures the delicate emotional landscape that unfolds between Kousuke and Natsume, childhood friends whose relationship is strained by the shadows of their past and the impending demolition of their shared home. The haunting atmosphere of the apartment complex, juxtaposed against the vast, turbulent ocean, serves as a metaphor for their internal turmoil, evoking a sense of both safety and existential peril.

As the narrative progresses, the floodwaters symbolize not only physical danger but also the rising tides of unresolved emotions. The tension between Kousuke and Natsume escalates, particularly over the old camera that embodies their shared memories and the weight of their grief. This conflict propels them into a surreal journey, where they must navigate both the literal and metaphorical currents of their relationship.
