In "Overflow," the narrative unfolds in a playful yet provocative atmosphere, where the boundaries of friendship and desire blur. Kazushi Sudou, a university student, finds himself at the center of an unexpectedly intimate scenario with his childhood friends, Ayane and Kotone Shirakawa. The emotional tension escalates when Ayane, feeling slighted over a forgotten treat, decides to take playful revenge by joining Kazushi in the bath. This setup creates a blend of comedic mischief and underlying romantic tension, reminiscent of works like "Kiss x Sis" and "Nisekoi," where the dynamics of youthful relationships often lead to humorous yet complicated situations.

Viewers are drawn into Kazushi's internal conflict as he navigates his burgeoning feelings for both sisters, evoking themes of jealousy, innocence, and the awkwardness of adolescence. This anime resonates particularly well with those who appreciate slice-of-life narratives infused with ecchi elements, where emotional stakes are heightened by comedic scenarios.
