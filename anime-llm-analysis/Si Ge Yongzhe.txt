The philosophical undertones challenge the notion of heroism, inviting audiences to reconsider what it means to save someone and the nature of companionship in a world filled with chaos.

This anime resonates with viewers looking for a light-hearted escape, humor that pokes fun at genre conventions, and a narrative that embraces the absurdities of life. Whether you're feeling whimsical or in need of a good laugh, "Si Ge Yongzhe" offers a delightful journey through a fantastical yet relatable landscape.
