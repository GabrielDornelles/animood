This two-episode OAV serves as a culmination of unresolved tensions, particularly focusing on Aya's confrontation with her sister Maya, which adds layers of emotional depth and familial conflict. The atmosphere is charged, oscillating between moments of relentless action and introspective drama, creating a dichotomy that keeps viewers engaged. The pacing is brisk during fight sequences, showcasing superhuman abilities and brutal confrontations, while slowing down to explore the characters' psychological struggles, reminiscent of "Tokyo Ghoul." Nagi's impending duel with Mitsuomi encapsulates the overarching theme of existential conflict, where the fight is not just physical but deeply tied to personal identity and honor.

The series resonates with viewers seeking a raw, gritty experience that balances adrenaline-fueled combat with emotional stakes.
