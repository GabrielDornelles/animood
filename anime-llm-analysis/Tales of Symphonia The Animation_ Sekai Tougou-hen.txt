The animation style complements this emotional depth, with vibrant landscapes contrasting with the darker themes of conflict and discrimination.

This anime speaks to those seeking a thoughtful exploration of character dynamics within a fantastical setting, perfect for viewers in a contemplative or nostalgic mood.
