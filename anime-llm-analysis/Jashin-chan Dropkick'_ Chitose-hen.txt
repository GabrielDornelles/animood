The narrative unfolds as a group of devils embarks on a comedic journey to Chitose, Hokkaido, showcasing a dynamic blend of gag humor and mythical elements. This light-hearted escapade is punctuated by the misadventures of Jashin-chan, whose reluctance to join the trip is overshadowed by her comedic antics, creating a playful atmosphere reminiscent of shows like "Konosuba" and "The Devil is a Part-Timer!"

As Jashin-chan inadvertently loses their hotel ticket to a mischievous deer, the story escalates into a frantic race against fallen angels, amplifying the absurdity and urgency. The anime's charming exploration of local culture, intertwined with its satirical take on bureaucratic processes, adds layers of depth, making it resonate with viewers who enjoy clever humor and social commentary.
