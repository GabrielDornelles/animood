In the bustling world of Tokyo Butei High School, where young detectives known as "Butei" are trained in the art of weaponry, the story unfolds with a blend of action, humor, and personal conflict. Kinji Tooyama, a seemingly ordinary second-year student, harbors a unique ability he wishes to keep hidden, striving for a peaceful existence amidst the chaos. However, fate intervenes when he becomes embroiled in a bombing incident, leading to an encounter with H. Aria Kanzaki, an exceptional S-Rank student whose strength and determination bring both admiration and rivalry.

Set against the backdrop of a secluded onsen retreat, the narrative shifts from intense action to a lighter, comedic atmosphere as the characters engage in playful banter and personal rivalries. The interactions among Kinji, Aria, and their classmates create a dynamic interplay of emotions, revealing underlying themes of identity, self-acceptance, and the struggle for normalcy in an extraordinary world. The pacing ebbs and flows, oscillating between moments of tension and levity, creating an engaging rhythm that captures the viewer’s attention.

Fans of series like "Sekirei" or "High School DxD" will appreciate the blend of action, romance, and character-driven humor that defines this anime. The psychological nuances of Kinji’s desire for anonymity against the backdrop of his extraordinary potential resonate with viewers seeking stories of self-discovery and the complexities of youth. The vibrant atmosphere, coupled with its mix of tension and lightheartedness, makes it a fitting choice for those in search of a blend of thrills and comedic escapades.
