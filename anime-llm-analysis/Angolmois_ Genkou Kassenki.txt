The narrative centers on Kuchii Jinzaburou and a band of exiled warriors, each with unique skills, as they confront overwhelming odds on the beleaguered Tsushima Island. The action is intense and visceral, showcasing not only the physical conflicts but also the psychological toll of warfare. The characters are intricately crafted, providing a diverse spectrum of personalities and motivations that deepen the viewer's engagement.
