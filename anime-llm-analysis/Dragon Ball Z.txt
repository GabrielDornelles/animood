This revelation of Gokuu's Saiyan heritage introduces a profound psychological conflict, as he grapples with his violent origins versus the compassionate life he has chosen. 

The atmosphere is charged with urgency and tension, expertly balanced by moments of camaraderie and humor among characters, reminiscent of classic shonen titles like "Naruto" and "One Piece."
