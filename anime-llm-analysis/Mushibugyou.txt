In the vibrant yet perilous world of Mushibugyou, viewers are thrust into an alternate Feudal Japan where colossal insect monsters wreak havoc on the streets of Edo. The narrative unfolds through the eyes of Jinbee, a young warrior burdened by the legacy of his father, a master swordsman. This coming-of-age tale is infused with a rich tapestry of action, comedy, and heartfelt drama, as Jinbee grapples with his inner fears and strives to prove his mettle among the formidable Insect Magistrates.
