This quest is not merely a search for celestial beauty; it becomes a profound journey into the intricacies of perception and the unseen. The eccentric Manabu Soutouin and the flamboyant Pretty Boy Detective Club, characterized by their striking aesthetics and peculiar charm, offer a vibrant backdrop against which themes of friendship, ambition, and self-discovery unfold.
